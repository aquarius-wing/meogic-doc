# Software Architecture

You can think of our software as an "operating system", a user-friendly thinking management OS.

- You can organize your thoughts on the whiteboard

- You can consolidate your ideas in documents

- You can link to your literature library in Zotero Connect

> Zotero is a literature management software used by many researchers.
